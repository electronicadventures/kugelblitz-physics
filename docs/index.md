# Kugelblitz Physics

Your companion for **QCAA Senior Physics** (2025 Syllabus v1.3).

---

## Course overview

Physics is a General senior syllabus consisting of four QCAA-developed units. Each unit has a notional time of 55 hours of teaching and learning, including assessment.

| Unit | Title | Topics |
|------|-------|--------|
| **1** | [Thermal, Nuclear & Electrical Physics](unit1/index.md) | Heating processes · Ionising radiation & nuclear reactions · Electrical circuits |
| **2** | [Linear Motion & Waves](unit2/index.md) | Linear motion & force · Waves |
| **3** | [Gravity & Electromagnetism](unit3/index.md) | Gravity & motion · Electromagnetism |
| **4** | [Revolutions in Modern Physics](unit4/index.md) | Special relativity · Quantum theory · The Standard Model |

Units 1 & 2 are completed first. Units 3 & 4 are studied as a pair, beginning in the final weeks of the year and continuing into the following year.

## How to use this site

Each unit has two ways to navigate:

**Lesson planner** — a sequential, lesson-by-lesson view that tracks the progression through the course. Each lesson row links to the relevant topic page and flags whether it is theory, practical, or assessment focused.

**Topic pages** — reference pages organised by subject. Each page contains theory notes with key formulas, embedded simulations and videos, worked examples, practice problems, and links to external resources.

## Assessment structure

| Assessment | Type | Weighting |
|------------|------|-----------|
| [IA1 — Data test](assessment/ia1-data-test.md) | Supervised test | 10% |
| [IA2 — Student experiment](assessment/ia2-student-experiment.md) | Individual experiment report | 20% |
| [IA3 — Research investigation](assessment/ia3-research-investigation.md) | Individual research report | 20% |
| [External exam](assessment/external-exam.md) | Combination response (Paper 1 + Paper 2) | 50% |
